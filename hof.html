HALL OF FAME

CRISTIANO RONALDO

Cristiano Ronaldo is a monumental figure in the history of Slayers FC, having laid the foundation for the club’s legendary status. As one of the club's original stars, Ronaldo played a pivotal role in its early success, leading the team to victory in both the first and third seasons while narrowly missing out on the second, where they finished as runners-up. His extraordinary skill, leadership, and relentless drive to win set the standard for the club's ethos. Ronaldo's contributions went beyond the goals and trophies; he instilled a winning mentality that has since become the backbone of Slayers FC. His presence during those formative years cemented his place as not just a player, but the cornerstone of the club's legacy.

ALEXANDER SORLOTH

Alexander Sørloth is a true legend for Slayers FC, a player whose impact transcended the pitch. Known for his towering presence and lethal finishing, Sørloth's consistent goal-scoring ability helped lift the club to new heights. His passion and dedication made him a fan favorite, always delivering in crucial moments with match-winning performances. Whether through a stunning long-range strike or a header in the dying minutes, Sørloth became the embodiment of the club's fighting spirit. His legacy at Slayers FC isn't just about the records he broke, but the passion and pride he instilled in every supporter.

ALESSANDRO BASTONI

Alessandro Bastoni is the heart and soul of Slayers FC, having been with the club since its inception and serving as captain for the majority of its illustrious history. His leadership from the backline has been instrumental in guiding the team to unprecedented success, winning 4 league titles, 2 league cups, and 4 super cups. Known for his defensive mastery, calm under pressure, and tactical intelligence, Bastoni has not only been a rock at the back but also an inspirational figure both on and off the pitch. His loyalty to Slayers FC and his ability to rally his teammates in the toughest moments have solidified his status as a club legend. Bastoni's enduring presence and unwavering commitment have made him the embodiment of Slayers FC's identity and ambition.

GIORGIAN DE ARRASCAETA

Giorgian de Arrascaeta is a revered club legend at Slayers FC, celebrated for his unparalleled achievement of winning every major trophy in the club’s history—5 league titles, 2 league cups, and 4 super cups. As the only player to have achieved this remarkable feat, his legacy is firmly etched in the annals of the club. GDA's ability to deliver in critical moments, often partnering with Sørloth to produce iconic late goals, showcased his extraordinary vision and playmaking skills. This dynamic super sub duo became synonymous with clutch performances, consistently turning games around with their chemistry. His contributions have not only added silverware to the club’s trophy cabinet but also ensured his place as one of the most influential and celebrated figures in Slayers FC’s storied history.
